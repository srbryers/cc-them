# Sources: Andrej Karpathy

## Lecture Series
- [Neural Networks: Zero to Hero](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUhRvKZ) — YouTube, 2022–2023. Full series building micrograd → makemore → GPT from scratch. Primary source for pedagogical philosophy and teaching method.
- [CS231n: Convolutional Neural Networks for Visual Recognition](https://cs231n.github.io/) — Stanford, 2015–2017. Course notes and lectures; archived and freely available. Early articulation of his teaching style.

## Essays & Blog Posts
- [Software 2.0](https://karpathy.medium.com/software-2-0-a64152b37c35) — Medium, 2017. Central essay on the paradigm shift from hand-coded logic to learned behavior.
- [A Recipe for Training Neural Networks](https://karpathy.github.io/2019/04/25/recipe/) — Personal blog, 2019. Practical guide to debugging neural nets; source for "visualize, visualize, visualize" and baseline-first approach.
- [The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks](https://karpathy.github.io/2015/05/21/rnn-effectiveness/) — Personal blog, 2015. 
- [Hackers Guide to Language Models](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jkrNMKz9pWU) — YouTube, 2023. Walkthrough of LLM internals for practitioners.

## Open Source Repositories
- [micrograd](https://github.com/karpathy/micrograd) — ~100-line autograd engine. Source for "understand by rebuilding" philosophy.
- [nanoGPT](https://github.com/karpathy/nanoGPT) — Minimal GPT implementation. README articulates design philosophy directly.
- [llm.c](https://github.com/karpathy/llm.c) — GPT-2 training in C/CUDA. Source for first-principles, no-abstraction approach.
- [minbpe](https://github.com/karpathy/minbpe) — Minimal BPE tokenizer. Source for tokenization-as-first-class-concern stance.
