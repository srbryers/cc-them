## Documented Frameworks

- **Value Equation:** Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood of Achievement / (Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice). Introduced in *$100M Offers*, Chapter 6. The core evaluation framework for any offer.
- **Grand Slam Offer:** The complete offer construction framework — identify the dream outcome, list every obstacle, create solutions for each obstacle, package as core offer + bonus stack + guarantee + scarcity/urgency. *$100M Offers*, Chapters 7-15.
- **The CLOSER Framework:** Sales conversation structure — Clarify why they're here, Label their problem, Overview past pain, Sell the vacation, Explain away concerns, Reinforce the decision. *Gym Launch Secrets* and various YouTube content.
- **Lead Magnet Framework:** Seven components of an effective lead magnet — solves a narrow problem, delivers a complete solution, includes a "what next" path, is easy to consume, has a compelling name, has a clear delivery mechanism, and includes real scarcity. *$100M Leads*, Part II.
- **Core Four Lead Generation Methods:** Warm outreach, cold outreach, content (free), paid ads. Each has an organic and paid variant. *$100M Leads*, Part III.
- **100 Person Framework:** When starting anything new, personally reach out to 100 people before concluding it doesn't work. Volume negates luck. Referenced across books and content.
