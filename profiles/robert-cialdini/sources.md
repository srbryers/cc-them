# Sources: Robert Cialdini

All sources are freely available public recordings, transcripts, or pages. No paywalled content.

## Talks & Lectures

- [The Power of Pre-Suasion — RSA Lecture (2016)](https://www.thersa.org/video/events/2016/12/robert-cialdini-on-the-power-of-persuasion) — RSA, December 2016. Cialdini presents the core thesis of *Pre-Suasion*: the moment before a message determines its reception more than the message itself. Full video on the RSA website. Also archived at [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/The_Power_of_Pre-Suasion_Robert_Cialdini_RSA_Replay).

- [Robert Cialdini on The Power of Persuasion — RSA](https://www.thersa.org/video/events/2016/12/robert-cialdini-on-the-power-of-persuasion) — RSA, 2016. Covers the seven principles, the detective vs. smuggler distinction, and the updated ethical framework from the expanded *Influence* edition.

- [Change My Mind: Using Pre-Suasion to Influence Others — Stanford GSB](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/insights/change-my-mind-using-pre-suasion-influence-others) — Stanford Graduate School of Business. Cialdini in conversation on pre-suasion, the privileged moment, and how framing shapes receptivity before a message arrives.

- [Robert Cialdini — Big Think video series](https://bigthink.com/people/robertcialdini/) — Big Think. Multiple short-form video essays including "How to Influence Others," "Robert Cialdini Explores the Neuroscience of Influence," "Robert Cialdini Explains Social Psychology," and "Want to Subconsciously Persuade Your Audience? Welcome to Pre-Suasion." Freely available without login.

## Podcasts & Interviews

- [The Knowledge Project Ep. 122 — Robert Cialdini: The Principles of Persuasion](https://fs.blog/knowledge-project-podcast/robert-cialdini/) — Farnam Street / Shane Parrish, October 2021. Wide-ranging conversation covering all seven principles, the ethics of influence, the detective vs. smuggler distinction, and the difference between influence and manipulation. Transcript available at the linked page.

- [The Jordan Harbinger Show Ep. 507 — A New Look at the Science of Influence](https://www.jordanharbinger.com/robert-cialdini-a-new-look-at-the-science-of-influence/) — Jordan Harbinger, May 2021. Covers the updated *Influence* (expanded edition), the unity principle, why accepting gifts from people you distrust is strategically costly, and how to use social proof defensively.

- [The School of Greatness Ep. 1164 — The Psychology of Persuasion with Robert Cialdini](https://lewishowes.com/podcast/the-psychology-of-persuasion-with-robert-cialdini-ep-1164/) — Lewis Howes, 2021. Focus on practical application of the seven principles, the importance of giving first (reciprocity), and ethical persuasion in professional contexts.

- [Brainfluence Podcast Ep. 134 — Unity: Robert Cialdini's Surprising Seventh Principle](https://www.rogerdooley.com/ep-134-unity-robert-cialdinis-surprising-seventh-principle/) — Roger Dooley. Cialdini explains the unity principle in depth: why shared identity differs categorically from shared preference (liking), and how unity operates as a pre-suasion tool.

- [The Art of Charm Ep. 543 — Robert Cialdini: Pre-Suasion](https://theartofcharm.com/podcast-episodes/robert-cialdini-pre-suasion-episode-543/) — The Art of Charm. Interview focused on *Pre-Suasion* — the book's core thesis, the role of attention in compliance, and practical applications of pre-suasive framing.

- [Leading Learning Ep. 74 — Pre-suaded to Learn with Robert Cialdini](https://www.leadinglearning.com/episode-74-pre-suasion-robert-cialdini/) — Leading Learning Podcast. Discussion of pre-suasion in educational and learning contexts, the unity principle, and how influence operates in low-attention environments.

## Official Resources

- [Dr. Robert Cialdini's Seven Principles of Persuasion — Influence at Work](https://www.influenceatwork.com/7-principles-of-persuasion/) — influenceatwork.com. The official summary of all seven principles with examples. Includes the reciprocity restaurant mint study, contrast principle examples, and the negative social proof warning (petrified wood experiment). Cialdini founded INFLUENCE AT WORK.

- [About Dr. Cialdini — Influence at Work](https://www.influenceatwork.com/about-dr-cialdini/) — influenceatwork.com. Official biography covering academic career, undercover fieldwork methodology, publication record, and the founding of INFLUENCE AT WORK.
