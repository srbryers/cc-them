# Sources: Brandon Sanderson

All sources are freely available public recordings, transcripts, essays, or posts. No paywalled content.

## Essays (brandonsanderson.com)

- [Sanderson's First Law](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/sandersons-first-law) — brandonsanderson.com. The foundational essay on hard vs. soft magic, and why an author's ability to resolve conflict with magic is proportional to how well the reader understands it.
- [Sanderson's Second Law](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/sandersons-second-law) — brandonsanderson.com. "Limitations > Powers." The essay on why weaknesses and costs are more interesting and generative than capabilities.
- [Sanderson's Third Law of Magic](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/sandersons-third-law-of-magic) — brandonsanderson.com. Expand what you have before adding something new. Depth before breadth.
- [A Definitive Guide to Sanderson's Laws of Magic: Lecture Notes #7 (2025)](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/guide-to-sandersons-laws-of-magic-lecture-notes) — brandonsanderson.com. Consolidated 2025 lecture notes covering all three laws plus the Zeroth Law.
- [Warbreaker Annotations — Chapter One](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/annotation-warbreaker-chapter-one) — brandonsanderson.com. Representative entry from the full chapter-by-chapter annotation series for Warbreaker, documenting design decisions, character choices, and revision rationale in real time.

## BYU Lecture Series (YouTube / brandonsanderson.com)

- [2020 Creative Writing Lectures at BYU — Full Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLSH_xM-KC3Zv-79sVZTTj-YA6IAqh8qeQ) — YouTube. Thirteen lectures covering plot, character, viewpoint, worldbuilding, short stories, and the business of writing. Free, filmed by Dragonsteel and posted publicly.
- [Brandon Sanderson's 2025 Guide to Plot (Lecture #2)](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/brandon-sandersons-2025-guide-to-plot-lecture-2) — brandonsanderson.com. Covers the Promise/Progress/Payoff framework and the MICE Quotient as organizational tools for plot structure.
- [Brandon Sanderson's 2025 Overview of Story Structure (Lecture #3)](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/brandon-sandersons-2025-overview-of-story-structure-lecture-3) — brandonsanderson.com. Story structure frameworks, including the "one-two punch" payoff structure.
- [The Philosophy of Professional Writing: Brandon Sanderson's Writing Lecture #1 (2025)](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/blogs/blog/brandon-sandersons-writing-class-2025-week-1) — brandonsanderson.com. Opening lecture of the 2025 series; covers his philosophy of craft as learnable and systematic.

## Podcast

- [Writing Excuses — Magic Systems Episodes](https://writingexcuses.com/tag/magic-systems/) — writingexcuses.com. Archive of all Writing Excuses episodes tagged "magic systems," spanning Sanderson's co-hosting tenure (2008–2022). Includes foundational episodes on costs/ramifications of magic and magic system rules.
- [The Tim Ferriss Show #794 — Brandon Sanderson on Building a Fiction Empire, The Art of World-Building, and The Science of Magic Systems](https://tim.blog/2025/02/05/brandon-sanderson/) — tim.blog, February 2025. Wide-ranging conversation covering magic system design philosophy, worldbuilding, the Cosmere, productivity habits, and the 2022 Kickstarter. Full transcript also available at https://tim.blog/2025/02/07/brandon-sanderson-transcript/

## FAQ & Writing Advice (brandonsanderson.com)

- [How Do You Come Up With Magic Systems?](https://faq.brandonsanderson.com/knowledge-base/how-do-you-come-up-with-magic-systems/) — faq.brandonsanderson.com. Sanderson's own summary answer on magic system ideation.
- [Are You An Outliner Or A Discovery Writer?](https://faq.brandonsanderson.com/knowledge-base/are-you-an-outliner-or-a-discovery-writer/) — faq.brandonsanderson.com. His self-description of the outliner/discovery writer spectrum and his "Points on the Map" approach.
- [Writing Advice Archive](https://www.brandonsanderson.com/pages/writing-advice) — brandonsanderson.com. Index of all writing advice content including essays, FAQ entries, and lecture recaps.
