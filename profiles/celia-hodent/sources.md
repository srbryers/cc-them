# Sources: Celia Hodent

All sources are freely accessible public recordings, transcripts, pages, or book listings. No paywalled or private sources are used.

## Books

- [The Gamer's Brain: How Neuroscience and UX Can Impact Video Game Design](https://www.routledge.com/The-Gamers-Brain-How-Neuroscience-and-UX-Can-Impact-Video-Game-Design/Hodent/p/book/9781498775502) — CRC Press / Routledge, 2017. Her primary text: the Game UX Framework, perception, memory, attention, onboarding, and engage-ability applied to game design.
- [The Psychology of Video Games](https://www.routledge.com/The-Psychology-of-Video-Games/Hodent/p/book/9780367493134) — Routledge, 2020. Accessible introduction to how psychology intersects with games as both makers and players; includes ethics, wellbeing, and potential harms.
- [What UX is Really About: Introducing a Mindset for Great Experiences](https://www.routledge.com/What-UX-is-Really-About-Introducing-a-Mindset-for-Great-Experiences/Hodent/p/book/9781032104447) — CRC Press, 2021. Concise statement of UX as a mindset rather than a toolkit; extends the framework beyond games.

## GDC Talks (Free / GDC Vault)

- [The Gamer's Brain: How Neuroscience and UX Can Impact Design (GDC 2015)](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1022310/The-Gamer-s-Brain-How) — GDC Vault. The foundational talk introducing perception, memory, attention, and the Game UX Framework to a game-developer audience. Slides and speaker notes also available at [celiahodent.com](https://celiahodent.com/video-game-ux-psychology/).
- [The Gamer's Brain, Part 2: UX of Onboarding and Player Engagement (GDC 2016)](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1022951/The-Gamer-s-Brain-Part) — GDC Vault. Deep dive on onboarding — how the brain learns, the forgetting curve applied to tutorials, common F2P onboarding failures, and Self-Determination Theory for engagement. Slides at [celiahodent.com](https://celiahodent.com/gamers-brain-ux-onboarding/).
- [The Gamer's Brain, Part 3: The UX of Engagement and Immersion / Retention (GDC 2017)](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1024055/The-Gamer-s-Brain-Part) — GDC Vault. Retention through cognitive science; how flow, motivation, and immersion can be designed for rather than hoped for. Covered in [Game Developer writeup](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/video-a-game-dev-s-guide-to-the-ux-of-engagement-and-immersion). Talk overview at [celiahodent.com](https://celiahodent.com/gamers-brain-part-3-ux-engagement-immersion-retention-gdc17-talk/).
- [Ethics in the Game Industry (GDC 2019)](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1025730/Ethics-in-the-Game) — GDC Vault (free). Reviews scientific evidence on game addiction, violence, loot boxes, and dark patterns; argues for an industry ethics framework. Slide deck also at [media.gdcvault.com (PDF)](https://media.gdcvault.com/gdc2019/presentations/Hodent_Celia_Ethics-in-the-Game-Industry.pdf).

## Podcasts & Interviews

- [Games, Psychology, and UX with Celia Hodent — GDC Podcast ep. 8](https://gdconf.com/news/games-psychology-and-ux-celia-hodent-gdc-podcast-ep-8-0) — GDConf.com, 2020. Wide-ranging conversation on UX vs. UI, affordance, challenge calibration, Fortnite examples, and why life is UX. Also on [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/episode/1oiwTxgQoooRQfU8WyHui2) and [Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/8-games-psychology-and-ux-with-celia-hodent/id1476405424?i=1000472359190).
- [Using UX Strategy to Put Players First — Enduring Play Podcast S1 E1](https://www.gamedevelopmentstrategyguide.com/enduring-play-season-1/using-ux-strategy-to-put-players-first-with-celia-hodent-phd-enduring-play-s1-e1/) — Game Development Strategy Guide, 2024. Covers UX strategy for game teams, the difference between usability and engagement, and how to build a UX practice from scratch.
- [Former Fortnite UX Lead Digs into Ethical Game Design](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/former-i-fortnite-i-ux-lead-digs-into-ethical-game-design) — Game Developer (Gamasutra), article/interview. Hodent on the ethics of monetization, dark patterns, and building a more ethical industry after leaving Epic.
- [Interview: Celia Hodent Talks Teaching UX and Ethics in Masterclass](https://gdconf.com/news/interview-celia-hodent-talks-teaching-ux-and-ethics-masterclass) — GDConf.com. On her GDC Masterclass, the challenges of teaching UX to developers, and the relationship between ethics and UX practice.

## Website & Case Studies

- [Celia Hodent — About](https://celiahodent.com/about/) — celiahodent.com. Primary biography: PhD background, career at Ubisoft Montreal, LucasArts, and Epic Games; consulting work from 2017.
- [Developing UX Practices at Epic Games](https://celiahodent.com/ux-practices-epic-games/) — celiahodent.com. Case study on building a UX function at Epic during Fortnite's development; covers how UX heuristics and playtesting were applied in practice.
- [Ethics in the Videogame Industry: A Mythbusting and Scientific Approach](https://celiahodent.com/ethics-in-the-videogame-industry/) — celiahodent.com. Enhanced written version of the GDC 2019 ethics talk; covers addiction research, dark patterns, and her case for an industry code of ethics.
- [Ethical Games — Learn More](https://ethicalgames.org/learn-more) — ethicalgames.org. The co-founded initiative for a Western game industry ethics code; background on the project's goals and principles.
- [Resources](https://celiahodent.com/resources/) — celiahodent.com. Curated list of Hodent's own talks, slides, articles, and recommended readings; useful index to her public output.
