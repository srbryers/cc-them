# Sources: Leil Lowndes

All sources are freely accessible public web pages, book listings, or recorded media appearances.

## Official Website

- [Official Bio — Leil Lowndes](https://www.lowndes.com/official-bio/) — lowndes.com. Primary source for career history, client list, media coverage, and endorsements from Larry King and Regis Philbin.
- [My Story — Leil Lowndes](https://www.lowndes.com/about/) — lowndes.com. First-person account of her childhood shyness, career journey through teaching, flight attending, Broadway, cruise directing, and radio hosting.
- [Communication Tips — Leil Lowndes](https://www.lowndes.com/communication-tips/) — lowndes.com. Blog-format articles documenting named techniques and principles: the YOU for THAT Substitution, the dangers of "but," body language micro-tips, and rapport-matching strategies.
- [Speaking & Training — Leil Lowndes](https://www.lowndes.com/speaking-training/) — lowndes.com. Full list of her corporate training programs: How to Talk to Anybody About Anything, Networking for Nerds, Powerful Communication Skills for Women, and others.
- [How to Talk to Anyone — Book Page](https://www.lowndes.com/howtotalk/) — lowndes.com. Official description of the 92-technique framework, including the organizing principle that successful people differ not in intelligence but in interpersonal skill.

## Books (publicly listed, freely browsable)

- [How to Talk to Anyone: 92 Little Tricks for Big Success in Relationships — Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/35210.How_to_Talk_to_Anyone) — Goodreads. Community ratings, reader annotations, and publisher description. Primary source for the book's scope and section structure (nine ways to make a first impression, fourteen ways to master small talk and body language, etc.).
- [Leil Lowndes — Author Page, Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/618.Leil_Lowndes) — Goodreads. Complete chronological book list with publication years: *How to Make Anyone Fall in Love with You* (1996), *How to Talk to Anyone* (1998), *How to Be a People Magnet* (2000), *Undercover Sex Signals* (2006), *Goodbye to Shy* (2006), *How to Instantly Connect with Anyone* (2009), *How to Feel Confident* (2009), *How to Talk to Anyone at Work* (2018).
- [Goodbye to Shy: 85 Shybusters That Work! — Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Goodbye-Shy-Shybusters-That-Work/dp/1511305185) — Amazon.com. Publisher description covering the book's scope: public speaking anxiety, party protocol, making friends, work situations, and intimate contexts. Source for the "Shybusters" framing and the graduated-exposure model applied to shyness.
- [How to Instantly Connect with Anyone — Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/How-Instantly-Connect-Anyone-Relationships/dp/0071545859) — Amazon.com. Publisher description of the 96-technique follow-up to *How to Talk to Anyone*, confirming continuity of the numbered-technique format across her catalog.
