# Sources: Lenny Rachitsky

## Newsletter
- [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/) — The largest product management and growth newsletter on Substack, with 700k+ subscribers. Primary source for his frameworks on growth, activation, PLG, launch strategy, onboarding, and startup benchmarks. Published weekly with deep-dive essays and operator guest posts.

## Podcast
- [Lenny's Podcast](https://www.lennyspodcast.com/) — Weekly long-form interviews with product leaders, founders, and growth operators. Guests include leaders from Duolingo, Notion, Figma, Slack, Spotify, Stripe, Airbnb, Reforge, and many others. Available on all podcast platforms and YouTube. Key episodes cover growth frameworks, PLG, activation, pricing, and company-building.

## YouTube
- [Lenny's Podcast YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@LennysPodcast) — Video versions of podcast episodes plus standalone clips on specific growth and product topics. High-signal short clips on activation, onboarding, and growth levers are particularly useful.

## Key Newsletter Posts & Frameworks
- [What is good retention?](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/what-is-good-retention-issue-29) — Benchmark data on retention rates across consumer and B2B categories. The canonical reference for "what good looks like."
- [How the biggest consumer apps got their first 1,000 users](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/how-the-biggest-consumer-apps-got) — Research across 100+ companies on early growth tactics, categorized by strategy type.
- [The most popular growth frameworks](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/the-most-popular-frameworks-for-growing) — Survey of growth frameworks used by top operators, including Reforge's growth loops, North Star metrics, and AARRR pirate metrics.
- [What is a good activation rate?](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/what-is-a-good-activation-rate) — Benchmark data on activation rates across product categories.
- [How to kickstart and scale a marketplace](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/how-to-kickstart-and-scale-a-marketplace) — Multi-part series on marketplace dynamics drawn from his Airbnb experience and interviews with marketplace founders.
- [The ultimate guide to onboarding](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/the-ultimate-guide-to-onboarding) — Framework for designing onboarding flows that maximize activation.
