# Sources: Sid Meier

## Books
- [Sid Meier's Memoir!: A Life in Computer Games](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/606762/sid-meiers-memoir-by-sid-meier/) — W. W. Norton, 2020. Primary source for design philosophy, specific decisions in Civilization and other titles, and personal reflections on decades of game development.

## GDC Talks
- [Interesting Decisions](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1018060/Interesting) — GDC 2012. The canonical talk on his core formulation. Free with GDC Vault free tier.
- [The Civilization Postmortem](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1014406) — GDC 2010.
- [Psychology of Game Design: Everything You Know Is Wrong](https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1015350) — GDC 2012. On player psychology, fun, and what designers get wrong.

## Interviews & Podcasts
- [Lex Fridman Podcast #Two-Minute Papers adjacent / assorted](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=sid+meier+interview) — Various.
- [Sid Meier interview with The Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2020/oct/17/sid-meier-civilization-games-memoir) — 2020. Memoir-related interview, covers design philosophy and career retrospective.
- [IGN: Sid Meier Interview](https://www.ign.com/articles/2012/03/09/gdc-2012-sid-meier-on-what-makes-a-great-game) — 2012. Post-GDC talk interview on interesting decisions.
- [Gamasutra / Game Developer Magazine interviews](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/search#q=sid%20meier) — Multiple profiles over two decades.
