# Sources: Rand Fishkin

## Books
- [Lost and Founder: A Painfully Honest Field Guide to the Startup World](https://www.amazon.com/Lost-Founder-Painfully-Honest-Startup/dp/0735213321) — 2018, Penguin Random House. Covers radical transparency, VC critique, building sustainable businesses. Not directly about SEO but establishes his philosophy on honest marketing and sustainable growth.

## Original Research & Data Studies
- [2024 Zero-Click Search Study](https://sparktoro.com/blog/2024-zero-click-search-study-for-every-1000-us-google-searches-only-374-clicks-go-to-the-open-web-in-the-eu-its-360/) — Core data: 58.5% of US Google searches end without a click, only 360/1000 clicks reach the open web. Based on Datos clickstream data.
- [Google API Leak Analysis](https://sparktoro.com/blog/an-anonymous-source-shared-thousands-of-leaked-google-search-api-documents-with-me-everyone-in-seo-should-see-them/) — May 2024. Primary takeaway: brand is the ultimate SEO signal, brand mentions function as "implied links."
- [AI Recommendations Are Inconsistent](https://searchengineland.com/ai-recommendations-inconsistent-fix-469250) — Study with 2,961 prompts showing LLM brand recommendations are highly inconsistent.
