# Sources: Rich Hickey

All sources are freely available public recordings, transcripts, or posts.

## Talks & Conferences

- [Simple Made Easy](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/Simple-Made-Easy/) — Strange Loop, 2011. The foundational talk on simple vs. easy, complecting, and the case for simplicity as a design goal.
- [The Value of Values](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/Value-Values/) — JaxConf, 2012. Values vs. places, epochal time model, information as simple.
- [Are We There Yet?](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/Are-We-There-Yet-Rich-Hickey/) — JVM Language Summit, 2009. Mutable state, epochal time model, identity vs. state vs. value.
- [Hammock Driven Development](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f84n5oFoZBc) — Strange Loop, 2010. Problem analysis, stepping away, the role of the subconscious in design.
- [Clojure Made Simple](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VSdnJDO-xdg) — Strange Loop, 2015. Walkthrough of Clojure's design decisions through the simplicity lens.
- [Design, Composition, and Performance](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/Design-Composition-Performance/) — Strange Loop, 2013. Analogy between music composition and software design.
- [Deconstructing the Database](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cym4TZwTCNU) — GOTO, 2012. Motivation for Datomic's design — information model, time, querying the past.
- [Spec-ulation Keynote](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oyLBGkS5ICk) — Clojure/conj, 2016. On semantic versioning, growth vs. breakage, and what it means to change a library.
- [Maybe Not](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YR5WdGrpoug) — Clojure/conj, 2018. On optional keys, open vs. closed specs, and the limits of static typing for information modeling.
- [Effective Programs - 10 Years of Clojure](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2V1FtfBDsLU) — Clojure/conj, 2017. Retrospective on Clojure's design and what's still needed.
- [Inside Transducers](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4KqUvG8HPYo) — Clojure/conj, 2014. Design of transducers — process transformation decomplected from source and destination.
